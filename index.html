FnF SIMPLE :  This type of planting stand is designed for beginner those who have a limited space and resources. It comes in multiples of 1'Hx2'Lx1'W with steps 1' , 2' and 3''.  

FnF CLEAN : This type of plant stand is an extended version of FnF SIMPLE, with overflow/excess water collection techniques. This keeps the terrace clean and dry.

FnF ENABLE : This type of plant stand is an extended version of FnF CLEAN, with a closed-loop electrical water drip system, available in the same dimensions.

FnF ENHANCE : This type of plant stand is an extended version of FnF ENABLE with an automated, IoT enabled water dripping system and enabled with water scheduler.
